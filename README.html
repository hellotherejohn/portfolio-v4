About

The site is coded by myself, John Karlsson, designer by day – amateur dev by night. Built in HTML and SASS using Sublime and CodeKit. It’s pretty much a vanilla build for now, but I’m looking at statamic, anchor cms or middleman for future releases.

Check it out at johnkarlsson.se

Technology

JS/CSS

  • Normalize.css – Get rid of browser quirks
  • jQuery – A common sight these days I guess
  • Animate.css – Ready-to-use animations by Dan Eden
  • Animsition.js – Fancy page transitions
  • WOW.js – Such scroll reveal
  • Retina.js – Automatically serving @2x if possible
  • smooth-scroll.js – Smooth anchor links
  • vide.js – Serving background video the easy way

Other

  • Pingdom – Uptime and Performance Monitoring
  • Cloudflare CDN – Speedy asset delivery (and their handy “always online” feature)
